Valentine's day

An annual holiday celebrated on February 14

Why did Valentine's Day appear?

Honoring St. Valentine – A priest who married lovers in secret.

Roman Festival – Inspired by Lupercalia, a love celebration.

Medieval Belief – Birds start mating on February 14th.

What can you give for Valentine's Day?

  • Romantic Dinner – at home or in a restaurant, the key is the atmosphere;
  • Personalized Gift – engraved jewelry, a photo book, or a custom phone case;
  • Experience Gift – a spa certificate, hot air balloon ride, escape room, or concert tickets;
  • Surprise Box – favorite sweets, love notes, and cute little gifts;
  • Jewelry or Accessories – a bracelet, watch, perfume, or a stylish wallet
